△ The Cathedral △

Basilisk! The temple the size of a planet


It was built in a time forgotten. It has long since abandoned it to the decay of time. The structure remains in a slowly eroding orbit of a binary star.


△ The Relics △

Price is paid for the mysteries


Pilgrims, treasure seekers, and religious sects have traveled across the galaxy to accept what the planet Basilisk has to offer.


△ Geist △

Demigod spirits wander ambivalent to humanity's presence


The octahedron planet’s only native life are monolithic demigods called geists. They wander Basilisk, uncaring about the human presence. Any attempts to interact with them have led to death or madness. Occasionally they will litter the planet with their spawn, who tend to murder and torture the pilgrims in occult rituals.


△ Empire △

Galaxy spanning Empire


Some of Empire’s feudal lords have taken an interest in the newly discovered Basilisk. These lords are no longer human. They are nothing more than capricious immortal synthetic intelligences that have systematically acquired galactic kingdoms. They make up a machinic court, casting their human subjects towards Basilisk.

△ The Book of The Octahedron △

Fragments from the first finders


The Book of The Octahedron is the name given to journal entries and other data collected from the first group to discover Basilisk. Many of these people are considered saints. Sects have formed around speculation and interpretation of the first human experiences on Basilisk.
